What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, improve user experience, and provide information to website owners.

We use only strictly necessary cookies to operate our website. These cookies are essential and do not require user consent. They include:

  • Functional Cookies: These cookies are used to provide features or services you have requested, such as remembering your cookie consent preferences.
