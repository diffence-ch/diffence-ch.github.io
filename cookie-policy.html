Privacy & Cookie Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains how we use cookies and handle any personal data in accordance with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Cookies

We use only essential cookies necessary for the operation, security, and performance of our website. No marketing or tracking cookies are used.

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that maintain your browsing session and are deleted once you close your browser.
  • Functional Cookies: Used to remember your preferences, such as cookie consent.
  • Cloudflare Security Cookies: To protect our site and ensure fast delivery, Cloudflare sets cookies like __cf_bm and __cfduid. These help distinguish humans from bots and prevent malicious traffic. They do not track you across other websites. See Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

Since only essential cookies are used, no cookie banner is displayed. By continuing to use the site, you consent to their use. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may reduce site functionality.

Privacy and Personal Data

We do not collect or process personal data automatically through this website. Any personal data you provide voluntarily (e.g., via email contact) is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

Your Rights

Under the FADP and GDPR, you have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Accessing your data
  • Correcting or deleting your data
  • Withdrawing consent for data processing where applicable

These rights apply only if personal data is processed.
