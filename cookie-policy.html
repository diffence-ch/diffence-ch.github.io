Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies to ensure the proper functioning of the site. Below, we explain what cookies are, how we use them, and your rights regarding their use.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the site owners.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use only necessary cookies on our website. These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They include:

  • Session Cookies: These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They are used to maintain the state of your session as you navigate the site.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies are necessary to provide features and services you have asked for, such as remembering your cookie consent preferences.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to the use of necessary cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings, but please note that disabling these cookies may affect the functionality of the website.
