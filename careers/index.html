Careers at Diffence.CH

At Diffence.CH, we are passionate about security and innovation. We are always on the lookout for talented individuals who share our vision of a safer digital world. Whether you're a seasoned security expert or just starting out in your career, we offer opportunities to grow, learn, and make a real impact.

Why Work With Us?

  • Innovative Environment: Be a part of cutting-edge projects and collaborate with industry-leading professionals.
  • Continuous Learning: Access to ongoing training, certifications, and opportunities to attend conferences.
  • Flexible Work Culture: We offer flexible hours and remote work options to help you balance your professional and personal life.
  • Impactful Work: Contribute to meaningful projects that make a difference in the security landscape.
  • Inclusive Culture: We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive work environment.

Open Positions

We are currently hiring for the following roles:

  • Security Engineer - Help us identify and mitigate security threats across our infrastructure.
  • DevSecOps Engineer - Integrate security practices into our development and deployment pipelines.
  • Security Researcher - Discover new vulnerabilities and contribute to our research initiatives.

How to Apply

If you are interested in joining our team, please send your resume and a cover letter to careers@diffence.ch. In your cover letter, be sure to include why you're passionate about security and what you hope to achieve at Diffence.CH.

We look forward to hearing from you!

Internships

We also offer internship opportunities for students and recent graduates who are eager to kickstart their careers in cybersecurity. If you're interested in an internship, please contact us at internships@diffence.ch.
